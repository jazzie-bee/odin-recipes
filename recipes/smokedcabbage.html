Smoked Cabbage

Description

Smoked cabbage is hands-off and a perfect side dish for whatever floats your boat. Pairs well with beef, pork, and chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 small head green cabbage, quartered
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup real bacon bits
