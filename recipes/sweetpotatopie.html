Sweet Potato Pie

Description

A Southern treat! This sweet potato pie recipe was shared with me by my father. It has been a long time favorite, and everyone who tastes it says it is the best they've ever had. This recipe makes 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 3 large sweet potatos
  • 2 sticks butter, softened
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup evaporated milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 ½ teaspoon allspice
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

Steps

  1. Peel your sweet potatos and cut them into ¼ pieces.

  2. Preheat oven to 350 degress F.

  3. Place them in a boiler with water to barely cover the potatos then cover pot with lid.

  4. Boil the sweet potat for 30 to 40 mintues or until tinder then drain excess water.

  5. Get a mixer and beat the sweet potatos until fluffy.

  6. Add butter and sugar then mix until incorporated.

  7. Add eggs and mix again.

  8. Add milk, spices and vanilla extract and mix until fully incorporated.

  9. Pour mixture into two unbaked pie shells and bake for 45 to 50 minutes.

  10. Serve warm and enjoy!

TIP:

Wrapping the pie crust edges in foil helps to prevent them from burning.
